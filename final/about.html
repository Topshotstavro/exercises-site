About This Prototype

This prototype website was created as part of a final project exercise. It demonstrates how to structure a multi‑page site, including a navigation menu, content regions, and a footer. The goal is to provide a simple yet complete example of a website prototype.

The images used here are original, computer‑generated abstract paintings. They serve as placeholders and can be replaced with real artwork in a complete site.
